About Khushi

Personal visa expertise, delivered across three cities.

Western World Education Consultants was built around one idea: every student deserves clear, honest guidance on their visa journey — not a sales pitch. Led by Khushi, our counselling is hands-on and personal, covering everything from shortlisting the right country and course to preparing a strong visa file and getting interview-ready.

With a growing presence in Chandigarh, Gurugram and Ahmedabad, Khushi and the Western World team work closely with students at every step, so nothing about the process feels confusing or out of reach.
